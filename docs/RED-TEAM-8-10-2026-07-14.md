## (1) PEDAGOGY — Verdict: PASS

- **Age-appropriateness (8-10 / Tier 2):** Vocabulary, sentence length and
  cognitive demand fit early-middle primary. Numeracy stays within ~0-40 with
  finger/pebble support offered; place value stays at tens/ones. Reading uses
  short, concrete, high-frequency words. Science uses one idea per card. All in
  range, and a genuine step up from the 6-8 band (two-digit vs. 0-20; sentences
  vs. first words; the real fact-vs-opinion lesson vs. the 6-8 labelled demo).
- **Sequence / scaffolding:** Strong. Numeracy models the make-ten and count-back
  strategies before asking for the answer, and builds place value → two-digit →
  word problems → money in order. Reading climbs word → sentence-completion →
  comprehension → production. Thinking establishes the checkability criterion
  first, then applies it, then separates "know" from "good guess". Every pack ends
  with an open, low-stakes `repeat-aloud` "your turn".
- **Cognitive load:** Low, controlled. Choice sets are 2-3. The `match` card runs
  one sub-round at a time. The child-facing card shows a picture/word + one Listen
  button + choices only — prompt/answer text stays behind the caregiver panel.
- **Constructive feedback / no shame:** Verified in-browser — a wrong tap shows a
  gentle "try again" (دوباره امتحان کن) with a brief flash and NO Leitner penalty;
  the correct tap advances and celebrates. Answer copy reframes error as learning
  and praises effort ("that is real reading", "you are a little scientist now").
  No timers, scores, streaks, or comparison anywhere.
- **Correctness of content:** Checked. Maths answers verified (8+5=13, 15−6=9,
  24=2 tens+4 ones, 23+14=37, 12+7=19, 20−8=12, 10−5=5). Science claims are true
  and age-honest (plants make food using sunlight; leaves catch light; a seed
  grows into a plant/tree; roots hold in soil and drink; fish→water, bird→sky).
  The evidence-vs-guess card correctly teaches that a reasonable explanation can
  still be a guess, not a certainty.

## (2) CHILD-SAFEGUARDING — Verdict: PASS

- **No fear / shame / pressure:** As above — gentle retry, no punishment, warm
  tone, no scary imagery.
- **Content safety:** Every example is neutral and universal — water, sun, birds,
  fish, trees, a garden, apples, bread, coins, days of the week. No politics, no
  religion, no nationalism, no violence, no gender restriction, nothing that could
  put a child in trouble at home. The money card uses generic "coins" (سکه) — no
  currency, no regime imagery. No stranger-danger framing.
- **Privacy / data:** No data collection introduced. The lane touched content,
  one render line, CSS, the manifest, the validator and the SW cache string — no
  network calls, no storage of child data, no analytics. COPPA/GDPR-K posture
  unchanged.
- **Cultural fit for Afghan children:** Dari-appropriate, widely-understood
  vocabulary (آفتاب، آب، باغ، درخت، ماهی، پرنده، آسمان، کتاب، خانه، گل، سکه);
  Persian numerals ۰-۹ (Dari standard); reading words are standard written school
  forms. Each pack keeps an honest `audioNote` that no audio is recorded yet.
