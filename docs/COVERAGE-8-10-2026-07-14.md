## How the band works today (baseline before this lane)

`content/packs.manifest.json` is the single source of truth. The 8-10 band was an
**honest empty shell**: `packs: []` plus a `preview` (fa/en/de) of the five topics
it promises — *Reading sentences · Adding & subtracting · Fact vs. opinion · Plants
& habitats · A map of the world*. The app shows that preview as "coming soon" until
real packs land. No invented content, no faked lessons — the charter's "defined

## Honest gaps after this lane (the roadmap, not fakery)

Delivering four genuinely-good packs this lane covers **literacy, numeracy,
critical-thinking and science** for 8-10. What is still open, stated plainly:

1. **Geography — "a map of the world" (promised in the preview).** Kept as an
   honest shell this lane. The picture library has no globe / land-vs-water / map
   icon, and a tile-sized world map with recognizable continents cannot be drawn
   *well* without either crude, junky art or new secular icon work (no borders, no
   flags). Per the house rule (REAL 90 > FAKE 100) it is better to ship four
   excellent packs and author geography properly next — with a clean globe + land
   + water icon set — than to rush a weak fifth. **Flagged for a follow-up lane.**
2. **Life-skills / health at Tier 2** — no 8-10 life pack yet (healthy food, teeth,
   sleep, germs, emotions have names). A charter Tier-2 cell; open.
3. **Digital literacy** — "not everything on a screen is true" (seed), kind vs.
   unkind online. A charter Tier-2 cell; not started.
4. **Arts & music** — rhythm, colours, a poem to recite. Softer priority; open.
5. **Breadth within shipped subjects** — numeracy still needs ×/÷, place value to
   1000, time, measurement; science still needs water cycle, weather, states of
   matter, Sun/Moon/Earth, simple machines; literacy still needs retell / first
   paragraph. This lane lays the first solid pack per subject, not the whole tier.
6. **History / human-rights** — genuinely *by charter design* concentrated at
   Tier 3 / Tier 4, so their near-absence at 8-10 is intended, not an oversight.
   Only gentle seeds belong here and can ride future thinking/life packs.
