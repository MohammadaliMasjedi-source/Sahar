## The 8 lessons

1. **How a headline lies** вАФ technically-true-but-misleading headlines; "up to", dropped context, FOMO.
2. **Happening together is not causing** вАФ reverse causation, hidden third cause, coincidence.
3. **Spotting a manipulated statistic** вАФ percent without a base number, truncated graphs, mean vs. median, tiny samples.
4. **Base rates** вАФ why a rare thing stays rare even after a "positive" sign (worked rare-illness example).
5. **How to check a source** вАФ who / how do they know / who gains / where else; reverse image search; forwarded вЙ† confirmed.
6. **Motivated reasoning** вАФ confirmation bias, identity-protective belief, "what would change my mind?".
7. **Logical fallacies you can spot** вАФ ad hominem, appeal to crowd/authority, false dilemma, slippery slope, straw man.
8. **How to change your mind (without losing)** вАФ falsification, steelmanning, idea-vs-person, updating as strength.
