# License — recommendation (Mo's final call)

Sahar is free forever and not-for-profit. The recommended posture is a **dual license**:

- **Code → MIT.** Maximally permissive so anyone — another NGO, another diaspora, another country's
  denied children — can fork the engine and build their own Sahar. The point is reach, not control.
- **Original content & learning packs → CC-BY-SA 4.0.** Free to use and adapt, must credit, and any
  derivative stays free under the same terms. This protects the work from being enclosed or sold.

**Third-party Open Educational Resources** drawn into packs keep **their own** licenses (CC-BY,
CC-BY-SA, CC-BY-NC, public domain), recorded per pack in the pack's `license`/`attribution` fields —
see [`docs/OSS-REUSE.md`](docs/OSS-REUSE.md).

> This is a recommendation. **The final license is Mo's decision.** When chosen, replace this note
> with the actual `LICENSE` (code) and `LICENSE-CONTENT` (packs) files.
